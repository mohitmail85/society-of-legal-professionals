Legal Conclave & Awards Program

Access of Justice to All - Accessible & Affordable

Join us for an insightful discussion with leading legal experts and celebrate excellence in the legal profession

About the Event

A legal conclave bringing together legal experts, jurists and academicians with the aim to put their views on the most recent issues of the society highlighting the need of proper implementation and evolvement of the existing laws.

A platform recognizing and encouraging the work of legal professionals by presenting them recognition awards in their respective field of work. The conclave features 2 morning sessions followed by lunch, 2 afternoon sessions, and concludes with an awards ceremony.

Selection Criteria & Process

Guiding Principles

Merit-based evaluation only

No award for sponsorship, advertisements, or affiliations

Independent review

Structured scoring, peer review, and jury deliberation

Confidentiality

All submissions reviewed under strict confidentiality

Conflict-of-interest safeguards

Jurors must recuse themselves where conflicts exist

No assured winners

Awards withheld if minimum benchmarks not met

Eligibility Requirements

Standing

Advocate enrolled with State Bar Council / registered law firm

Experience

Min. 15 years PQE (5 years for "Rising Star")

Compliance

No pending disciplinary proceedings

Time Period

Work within defined assessment year
