CRITERIA FOR SELECTIONS AWARDS
We will Publish our principles on website and nomination brochure, which are as follows:
1.	Merit-based evaluation only
No award is given for sponsorship, advertisements, or affiliations.
2.	Independent and multi-tier review
Evaluation is conducted through structured scoring, peer review, and jury deliberation.
3.	Confidentiality and ethical compliance
All submissions are reviewed under strict confidentiality norms.
4.	Conflict-of-interest safeguards
Jurors must recuse themselves where any conflict exists.
5.	No assured winners
Awards may be withheld if minimum benchmarks are not met.

Award Eligibility & Qualification Thresholds
Before scoring begins, nominees must satisfy minimum eligibility criteria:

Parameter	Requirement
Standing	Advocate enrolled with a State Bar Council / registered law firm
Experience	Minimum 15 years PQE (except ‚ÄúRising Star‚Äù categories- Min. 5 years)
Compliance	No pending disciplinary proceedings
Time Period	Work performed within the defined assessment year
Disclosure	Declaration of accuracy and ethical compliance
Note: Only nominations clearing this stage will move to scoring.
