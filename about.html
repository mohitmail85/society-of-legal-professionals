About Us

Who We Are

The Society of Legal Professionals is a voluntary, non-profit organization dedicated to serving India's legal professionals. We are an all-inclusive platform working to develop a united front of legal professionals across the nation.

Our organization focuses on capacity building through national and international engagements while providing policy guidance on critical legal and social issues affecting our communities.

It further aims to bring all the legal professionals, who work in their respective fields making contribution in either formulation of any policy, rule, law, statute etc. or executing the same, under one umbrella.

Registered as a Society under the Society Registration Act (1860), we maintain the highest standards of transparency and accountability in all our operations.

Our Vision

To create a unified, empowered community of legal professionals who contribute to justice, equality, and social progress across India.

Organizational Structure

Patron

Distinguished legal luminary providing guidance, vision, and support to the organization's mission and strategic direction.

President

Chief executive officer leading the organization, setting strategic direction, and representing the Society in all official capacities.

Secretary

Manages official correspondence, maintains records, coordinates meetings, and ensures compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

Treasurer

Oversees financial management, budgeting, accounting, and ensures proper utilization of funds in accordance with the Society's objectives.
