Who We Are

The Society of Legal Professionals is a voluntary, non-profit organization dedicated to serving India's legal professionals. We are an all-inclusive platform working to develop a united front of legal professionals across the nation.

Our organization focuses on capacity building through national and international engagements while providing policy guidance on significant legal and social issues affecting our communities.

Registered as a Public Charitable Trust under India's Registration Act (1908), we maintain tax-exempt status and CSR eligibility, ensuring transparency and accountability in all our operations.

Our Vision

To create a unified, empowered community of legal professionals who contribute to justice, equality, and social progress across India.
